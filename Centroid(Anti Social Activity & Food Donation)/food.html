About Us



Centroid is a non-profit, food rescue organisation that bridges the gap between hunger and excess food. We connect the restaurants with a NGO. The restaurant can now contact the ngo and donate the leftover food, which will be delivered to the needy and hungry.We aim to bring benefits across the spectrum of society and help provide access to food to those who don’t have adequate means to access it. To bring a social change in every individual in-order to reduce food waste and to make the World Hunger Free.
